Welcome to the project page of the Wild Pointers!

This is the webpage of a repository created by the students of Budapest University of Technology and Economics for an assignment connected to the course: Software Laboratory 4

The game

This is basically a Tower Defense game in which you have to defend a mountain (Mordor) from the filthy elves, dwarves, humans and hobbits who are trying to reach it and toss the One ring into it. If they succeed you loose, if you kill all of them you win. The aim is to build towers in order to slow down and kill the enemy.

Good luck on your journey:)
